How to reach us
Chat with us on Slack

If you are looking for a more interactive way to communicate with us, drop in to our Slack channel to ask questions, share ideas, or just hang out and chat about all things Microclimate. If you have not previously used the ibm-cloud-tech slack instance, click here to request an invite.

Go to Channel
Bugs or recommendations

Found a bug? Have a recommendation on something we can do to improve the Microclimate experience? We opened up shop on GitHub, where you can notify us by creating an issue. And while our moving vans haven’t all arrived yet, they are all in transit. In the meantime, we have enough of our project in place to start working with you. Help us help you!

Go to GitHub
Need answers?

While we put a lot of effort into making Microclimate easy to use, we know there will always be questions. If you have something you need help with, post your question to Stack Overflow to leverage the minds and experiences of an army of developers…including ours!
